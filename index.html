My Story

My software development journey started when I built my first website as a kid — a fan page for the Nickelodeon show Rugrats — with the help of various online tutorials and by scanning the source of other sites I admired. My interest in software followed me into high school, where I helped maintain the district website and built an application to manage a class-wide game of Assassin using PHP and MySQL.

After college, my journey took a detour while I pursued writing, but I never stopped coding during that period. Mostly for myself at first — I enjoyed the problem solving aspect and how easily I could automate day-to-day tasks, e.g. comparing the price of books across different vendors or generating a daily news feed and sending it to my Kindle — but gradually I took on more freelance work as well, leveling up my skills along the way. This eventually inspired me to switch over to coding full time, and I've been loving what I do ever since.
