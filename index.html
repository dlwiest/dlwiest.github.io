My Story

My journey in software development began as a child when I built my first website — a fan page and info repository for my favorite Nickelodeon show — with the help of various online resources and by scanning the source of other sites I admired. This interest followed me into high school, where I helped maintain the district website and developed an application using PHP and MySQL to manage a class-wide game of Assassin.

After college, my journey took a detour to pursue an interest in writing, but I continued to hone my coding skills, driven by a love for problem solving component and the efficiency of automating common tasks like comparing the price of books across vendors or aggregating a daily news feed and sending it to my Kindle. As my projects gained attention, I started acquiring freelance clients, and before long, my lifelong hobby had transformed into my main source of income. This led me to commit fully to software development, and I've loved what I do ever since.

I'm a full stack web developer with a special passion for front end engineering. If you'd like to build something together, I'd love to hear from you!
